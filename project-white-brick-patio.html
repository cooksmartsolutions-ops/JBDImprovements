Patio Cover Gable Roof Cedar Trusses

A large gable-roof patio cover built on a white painted brick home, featuring heavy cedar posts, decorative king-post trusses, and tongue-and-groove ceiling. This project captures the full build process — from unstained framing through staining to the rich, dark-stained finished product. The covered area extends the living space with a concrete slab and sliding glass door access to the interior.
