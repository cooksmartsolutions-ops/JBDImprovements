Our Story

Driven by Passion,
Defined by Quality

JBD Improvements LLC was founded on a simple belief: every homeowner deserves a beautiful, functional outdoor space — and every project deserves to be built right.

Over the past decade, we've grown from a small patio cover company into a full-service construction and renovation team, serving hundreds of homeowners across Dallas, Fort Worth, and the surrounding communities.

We bring the same energy and attention to detail to every project — whether it's a simple patio cover or a complete outdoor kitchen and living space transformation.
